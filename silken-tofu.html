Silken Tofu

Silken tofu is a wonderful quick-prep dish that pairs perfectly with rice. It can be eaten right after preparing or kept in the fridge for up to 4 days. Personally I like to keep this and gaji-namul on hand for busy appointment days when I know I'll need something filling to eat but won't want to cook.

  • 1 package silken tofu or soft tofu

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar or honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes) or red pepper flakes, optional
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 green onion chopped
  • 1/2 chili pepper chopped, optiona
